Kevin Anderson Web 250 - Brand

For my brand, I wish to continue development of my WEB 110 project, virtualpcbuilder.com

The concept is to provide a web portal where computer hobbyists can assemble a parts list for a home built computer, and compare prices for different computer setups. The portal also provides how-to articles, blog posts, and hardware reviews for new and up-coming computer hardware.

Last semester I used a color palette based on a bright blue circuit board background. I like the circuit board theme, and I want to somehow integrate it into the next revision of virtualpcbuilder.com. I am not sure exactly what palette I like best, so for now I will show several samples based on various sample images.

The wesite colorhunter.com was used to automatically generate the palettes below from each sample image. Additional work will be needed to find complimentry colors for these base samples.
